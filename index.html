About Me

HI I'm Deadpool, or in my real name Wade Winston Wilson.
I am a fictional character (and I know it) in an American comic books published by Marvel Comics.
My creators are the writer Fabian Nicieza and artist/writer Rob Liefeld,
My first appearance is in The New Mutants #98 (cover-dated February 1991).
Initially they made me a supervillain in my first appearance in The New Mutants and later in issues of X-Force,
but later I developed into my more familiar antiheroic personality.
I am (definitely) a disfigured mercenary, and I have the superhuman ability of an accelerated healing factor and physical prowess.
At last you all may know me as the "Merc with a Mouth" because of my tendency to talk and joke constantly, including breaking the fourth wall
(between me and the audience) as a humorous effect and running gags.

This is an example of the differences between Web1, Web2 and Web3:
We run the query "Drugs side effects":

  • In Web1 we will get static websites with explanations, and related data.
  • In Web2 we can use Google to search the query and we will get a lot of related results, like side effect of a specific drug,   side effect on other organism (like animals). Not all the website that returns are relevant to us.
  • In Web3 we can search the query on a designated data-base (like one that holds all side effect for every drug)
      and probably get result and website that are very relevant to the query.
